A Haven on the Cumberland

Nashville's premier
full-hookup RV park.

Two Rivers RV Park sits two miles from the Grand Ole Opry, on a quiet bend of the Cumberland River — welcoming RVers to Nashville, Tennessee since 1972.

As Music Valley's premier full-hookup RV park, we offer 50-amp pull-through RV sites sized for the longest Class A motorhomes, level 30-amp back-in RV sites for travel trailers and 5th wheels, dependable Wi-Fi, HD cable, and a paved patio at every site. Add a heated pool, a fenced dog park, and live entertainment under the hardwoods, and Two Rivers Campground becomes your true Nashville RV-park home base for Music City.

The Resort

Mature shade.
Easy level pads.

This is the south end of the park on a Tuesday in May — a 5th wheel and a 40-foot Class A on neighboring full-hookup pull-throughs, gravel pads, picnic tables, and the kind of mature shade trees you only get from fifty years of staying put.

No tight turns. No tilted pads. Just easy pull-ins and full hookups under the hardwoods.

Amenities

Amenities Abound.

From the heated swimming pool and fenced dog park to live entertainment under the hardwoods — every amenity is included with every site, every night.

Free Wi-Fi

Park-wide network — strong enough to stream the Opry from your awning.

Free Coffee

Hot pot at the office every morning. Decaf brewed alongside.

HD Cable

HD cable hookup at every RV site — bring your awning chair and the remote.

Electric Hook-Ups

50-amp and 30-amp service at every site, tested and reliable.

Bathhouses

Clean, tiled bathhouses with private showers — open 24 hours.

Laundry

Coin-op washers and dryers on the grounds — soap available at the office.

Dog Park

Fenced run on the east side. Treats and water bowls at the office. Leash on, of course.

Playground

Wood-framed playground with swings and a slide — keeps the kids busy.

Swimming Pool

Heated family pool, fenced and clean, open Memorial Day through October.

Game Room

Pinball, pool table, classic arcade. The kids will be fine for an hour.

Small Meeting Room

Cozy on-site meeting space — perfect for caravan rallies and family gatherings.

Groceries & Souvenirs

On-site store with camp essentials, ice, snacks, and Two Rivers souvenirs.

Location

In the heart of Music Valley.

Two miles from the Grand Ole Opry. Fifteen minutes to downtown Broadway. Ten minutes to Percy Priest Lake. The rare Nashville address where you fall asleep to crickets and wake up to a steel guitar.

You'll never have to drive far for the good stuff.

The Grand Ole Opry is two miles north. Downtown Broadway is fifteen minutes south. The General Jackson showboat docks within walking distance. Percy Priest Lake is ten minutes east.
